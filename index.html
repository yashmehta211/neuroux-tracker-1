Zapier Setup
1. Go to zapier.com → Create Zap
2. Trigger: Otter → New Recording
3. Connect your Otter account
4. Action: Webhooks by Zapier → POST
5. URL: paste the webhook URL above
6. Payload: JSON with fields title, summary, action_items, created_at
7. Turn on your Zap
